Loan Terms & Conditions

Please read carefully before proceeding

1. Loan Agreement

By applying for a loan through Airtime & Loan Solution Kenya, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions. This agreement constitutes a legally binding contract between you (the borrower) and the lending partner.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a loan, you must:

  • Be a Kenyan citizen or resident aged 18 years or above
  • Have a valid Kenyan National ID
  • Have an active Safaricom M-Pesa registered phone number
  • Provide accurate personal and employment information
  • Have no outstanding defaulted loans with any lending partner

3. Loan Amounts and Terms

Loan amounts range from KES 4,000 to KES 15,000 with repayment periods of 60 to 100 days. Interest rates and fees vary by loan product and are clearly disclosed before loan acceptance.

4. Savings Deposit

A refundable savings deposit is required to activate your loan. This deposit serves as collateral and demonstrates your commitment to repayment. The savings deposit amount varies by loan product and is deducted from the loan principal before disbursement.

5. Disbursement

Upon successful completion of the savings deposit, the loan amount (minus the savings deposit) will be sent directly to your registered M-Pesa number within minutes.

6. Repayment

You agree to repay the full loan amount plus any applicable interest on or before the due date. Repayment must be made via M-Pesa to the provided paybill number. Late payments may result in:

  • Additional late payment fees
  • Negative credit bureau reporting
  • Collection activities
  • Forfeiture of savings deposit

7. Data Privacy

By submitting your application, you authorize us to collect, store, and process your personal information for loan assessment and servicing purposes. Your data will be shared with credit bureaus and lending partners as necessary.

8. Default and Collections

Failure to repay your loan on time constitutes default. In case of default, we reserve the right to contact you and your emergency contacts, report to credit bureaus, and engage collection agencies.

9. Cancellation

Once a loan is disbursed, it cannot be cancelled. The full repayment amount remains due regardless of whether you use the funds.

10. Amendments

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any modified terms.
